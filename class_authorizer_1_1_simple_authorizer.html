Authorizer\SimpleAuthorizer::registerPolicies (   $policies)

Associates resource types and actions with policy lists.

See "Combining Policies" section in README.md for details on how policy lists work.

May be called multiple times to register more policy associations.

Parameters
$policiesTwo-level associative array mapping resource types and actions to policy lists, e.g. "document" => ["read" => "allow", "write" => "owner"]
